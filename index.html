Formula One (1950 - 2023)

Formula One (F1) is one of the world's most prestigious racing competitions. Races take place in multiple locations around the world with millions of viewers and various sponsors participating. Each team engineers their own F1 car in which their drivers race around a circuit. Lets see how the fastest lap times differ between different circuits and where do they tend to be hosted?
